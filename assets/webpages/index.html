WHAT MAKEUP GOES FIRST?

Applying primer before applying any other makeup is essential if you want your makeup to last all day and still look great. Primers are designed to help your makeup stay as long as possible by smoothing out the skin, filling in small lines and wrinkles, and creating a blank canvas for your other cosmetics to go on top of. Lightweight and oil-free primer is ideal.
